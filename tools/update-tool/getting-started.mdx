## Getting Started

### Step 1: Access the Tool

Visit the **CSC Update Tool** at your designated URL. You'll see a welcoming interface showcasing the platform ecosystem.

### Step 2: Sign In

You have two convenient authentication options:

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Magic Link (Email)">
<Steps>
<Step title="Request Magic Link">
  Click **"Get Magic Link"** and enter your email address
</Step>
<Step title="Check Your Email">
  Click **"Send Magic Link"** and check your email
</Step>
<Step title="Click Secure Link">
  Click the secure link in your email to authenticate
</Step>
<Step title="Automatic Login">
  You'll be automatically logged in and ready to contribute
</Step>
</Steps>
</Tab>

<Tab title="GitHub Authentication">
<Steps>
<Step title="Connect GitHub">
  Click **"Continue with GitHub"**
</Step>
<Step title="Authorize Application">
  Authorize the CSC Update Tool application
</Step>
<Step title="Automatic Redirect">
  You'll be redirected back and automatically logged in
</Step>
</Steps>
</Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
**First-time users**: Don't worry about creating an account - both methods will automatically create your profile!
</Note>
