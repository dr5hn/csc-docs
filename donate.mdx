</Info>

## Questions About Donating?

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Is my donation tax deductible?">
  Tax deductibility depends on your jurisdiction and the payment method used. GitHub Sponsors may provide tax benefits for eligible organizations. Consult your tax advisor for specific guidance.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do you use the donations?">
  All donations go toward project sustainability: server costs (40%), data verification (30%), development time (20%), and community support (10%). We publish quarterly financial reports for full transparency.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I sponsor specific features?">
  Yes! Corporate sponsors can influence our development roadmap and prioritize specific features or improvements. Contact us to discuss custom sponsorship arrangements.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you offer commercial licenses?">
  The Country State City API is free for all use cases, including commercial applications. We don't require licensing fees, but appreciate voluntary contributions to support the infrastructure.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
